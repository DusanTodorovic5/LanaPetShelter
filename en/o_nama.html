Lana Pet Shelter is a civil society organization managing animal protection , fighting for animal rights.

Why us?

Lana Pet Shelter is a shelter for the protection of the abandoned, both healthy and sick, disabled and old animals, which is located in Belgrade. The asylum was established 20 years ago and was formally registered as an association for the protection of animals. It was founded by Marija Marković independently, without anyone's help, and today about 400 animals live in the shelter. Although it has existed for over 20 years, asylum is fought for every day dry existence, and the biggest problem is food, because, in order to feed all animals, over 100 kilograms of food is required per day. Another problem is winter which is yet to come, so the animals also need shelter from the cold and wind. It is for these reasons that an action was launched to provide assistance to the shelter. The website has helped a lot the further progress of asylum.

Our awards

As far as adoption is concerned, the past year was considered to be extremely successful, because more than 50 dogs have found owners under our protection. For that reason, our asylum won a prestigious award at a conference in Munich called "Find your pets". Awards were received for exceptional tidiness and cleanliness, fine treatment of animals as well as an impressive number of adopted animals in just one year. The award for "The most tidy asylum in Serbia" was also received.
Attention is drawn to the fact that the site has greatly contributed to the promotion of asylum! The plan is to apply for the competition, which will be held on June 20, 2023, when the asylum will be significantly improved.
